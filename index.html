Admission Process

We welcome students of exceptional potential, character, and drive. Our admissions process is designed to be clear, fair, and supportive.

Step 1 — Anytime
Submit Online Enquiry
Complete the enquiry form or contact our admissions office directly. We will send you the school's information and guide you through the next steps.
Step 2 — Rolling Basis
School Visit & Assessment
Book a school visit. Students attend a half-day session and complete a brief written assessment in English, Maths, and Science.
Step 3 — Within 2 Weeks
Interview & Transcript Review
A 30-minute interview with the Head of Admissions. Previous school transcripts and recommendation letters are reviewed.
Step 4 — 5 Business Days
Offer & Acceptance
Successful applicants receive a formal offer letter. Upon acceptance, pay the non-refundable registration fee to secure your place.
Step 5 — Term Start
Orientation & Enrolment
New students attend an orientation day, meet their class teacher, get shown around the school, and are introduced to school rules and routines before the term begins.
